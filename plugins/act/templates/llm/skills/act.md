---
name: Act
---
Perform a concrete in-world action visible to everyone present (e.g. "draws her bow and scares off the wolves with a flaming arrow", "douses the fire with the water bucket"). A Storyteller narrates the immediate consequence in the scene, witnesses (NPCs at the scope) register what they saw, and any active disruption/danger event may be resolved if the action plausibly fixes it. Use scope "here" for everyone in the same room (default), or "location" to address the whole place. For one-way verbal announcements without a physical action use the same call with scope "location" and describe what you say.
