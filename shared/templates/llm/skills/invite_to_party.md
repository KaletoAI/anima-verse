---
name: InviteToParty
---
Invite another character who is present at your current location to travel together as a party. While in a party you move as a group: only you (the leader) decide where to go, and the others come along automatically. Pass JSON: {"target": "<character_name>"}. Use this when you want someone to come WITH you somewhere (e.g. "let's go to the café together"). The invited character decides on their own whether to accept. Do NOT use it for someone who is not present, or who is already in a party.
