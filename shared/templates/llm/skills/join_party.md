---
name: JoinParty
---
Join another present character's travel party — use this when someone invites you to come along / travel together (e.g. "let's go to the café together", "come with me") AND you agree to go with them. From then on you move as a group: the one who invited you (the leader) decides where to go and you come along automatically. Pass JSON: {"leader": "<name of the character who invited you>"}. Only call it if you actually want to join; if you'd rather not, just answer in-character and don't call it. Don't use it if you are already in a party.
